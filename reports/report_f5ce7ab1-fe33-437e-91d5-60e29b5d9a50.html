Executive Summary

This BI dashboard provides a comprehensive and integrated view of supplier performance and risk, offering critical insights to optimize supply chain management and drive strategic decision-making. By consolidating key metrics such as Total Suppliers, various risk categories (High, Medium, Low), and an Average Supplier Risk Score, the platform enables proactive identification and mitigation of potential supply chain disruptions and compliance challenges. Concurrently, the inclusion of On-Time Delivery Percentage and Quality Rating offers a clear picture of operational efficiency and the quality of goods or services received, directly impacting downstream processes and customer satisfaction. The ability to segment this data by Business Unit and individual Supplier allows for granular analysis, facilitating targeted interventions and fostering more effective supplier relationship management. Stakeholders can quickly identify underperforming or high-risk suppliers, pinpoint areas for quality improvement, and assess the financial impact through Purchase Spend analysis. This multidimensional approach empowers departments to understand their specific supplier landscape and make data-driven decisions tailored to their operational needs. Ultimately, this dashboard serves as an invaluable tool for enhancing overall business resilience and achieving cost efficiencies. By providing a holistic perspective on supplier health, performance, and financial contribution, it supports strategic sourcing initiatives, helps reduce risk exposure, improves product/service quality, and optimizes expenditure across the organization. Leveraging these insights will lead to stronger supplier partnerships and a more robust, efficient, and reliable supply chain.
